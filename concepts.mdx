<Accordion title="Channel">
  A sales platform (e.g., Shopify, Amazon) connected to ShipBob.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Reference ID">
  A unique identifier for orders or shipments from your system.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Order">
  A customer's purchase request sent to ShipBob for fulfillment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shipment">
  The physical delivery of an order, which may include multiple items.
</Accordion>
